# Patches Orchestration — 2026-02-24T07:27:28Z

**Status:** Complete  
**Deliverables:** Turn-based combat with 3 classes, 9 abilities, 4 AI behaviors, 6 enemy types, bosses

## What was done
- Created `src/combat.js` with turn-based combat engine
- Implemented 3 classes (Warrior, Rogue, Mage) with 3 unique abilities each (9 total)
- 4 AI behaviors: aggressive, flanker, cautious, ranged
- 6 enemy types: rat, slime, orc, wraith, dark_mage, dragon
- Boss enemies: Ogre King (floor 5, 2.5x HP/1.8x ATK), Ancient Dragon (floor 10)
- Damage formula: `(attack + weapon - defense) × variance(0.8–1.2), min 1`
- Status effects refresh duration (no stacking)

## Integration points
- Registers hooks: `playerAction`, `enemyAction`, `statusTick`, `cleanup`
- Reads Entity.stats, abilities from CLASS_BASE_STATS, CLASS_ABILITIES (constants.js)
- Uses Tile.entityId for occupancy; bump-to-attack on movement
- Calls items.js for loot drops (40% health potion per enemy death)

## Key design
Greedy pathfinding (single-step nearest) sufficient for dungeon scope. Auto-targeting abilities for keyboard-only UI.
